Amendment and Cancellation policy

In case of cancellation, various charges will be taken depends on the type of services have to be canceled.

You can cancel domestic & international flight tickets online by login to My Bookings on SwipeCell.in. Along with airline's cancellation charges, an amount of 250 Rs. per passenger/per sector for domestic and 750 Rs. per passenger/per sector for international air tickets will be taken by SwipeCell.in as cancellation service charges.

For offline cancellation of these tickets, call our customer support center at 8111892447 (From All Networks). Same cancellation charges will be applied.

In case of special fares and other specific situations, fares are non-refundable.


***Fare refund policies are subject to vary as per the policies of airlines. Consequently, it becomes necessary to understand the terms and conditions of airlines before booking air tickets.

To cancel the tickets within 24 Hrs of the journey, contact the Airlines directly and then, send the cancellation request to our website for refunds.

In case of direct cancellation through airlines, refunds will be made as per the airlines' charges along with our service charges as mentioned above.

Post departure of flights & in case of No Show, we don't entertain any cancellation request

During Peak-Seasons that may be different for different locations, cancellation policy of hotels and air tickets can vary. We follow specific cancellation policy these days and refund charges may differ heavily.


Cancellation Charges for Holiday Packages:

If you cancel your holiday prior to the 30 days or more of the date of departure, initial deposit amount or 25% of the tour cost (whatever is more) will be taken as cancellation charges.

If you cancel your holiday between 29 days and 15 days prior to the date of departure, 50% of the tour cost will be taken as cancellation charges.

If you cancel your holiday between 14 days and 8 days of the tour, 75% of the tour cost will be taken as cancellation charges.

If you cancel your holiday within 8 days of the tour, 100% cancellation charges will be applied and no refunds will be given.


****These cancellation charges can vary as per the season or other conditions.

It is mandatory to contact SwipeCell.infor all refunds if booking is done through it. Refund against the cancellation of air tickets will be credited directly in your account. In case of Credit Card, you will receive the refunds in the same credit card. Refund process may take maximum 2-3 working days due to banking procedures. In case of Net Banking, it may take 7-8 working days.

Note: Processing times for cancellation and refund requests may vary.

If there is unavailability of services, you will receive total refund except the transaction fees.


Refunds against the Cancellation of Hotel Reservation:

Refunds against the cancellation of the hotels depend upon the cancellation policies of the particular hotels. Apart from the cancellation charges levied by the hotels, we charge a negligible amount of Rs. 300 as cancellation service charges for each room.
